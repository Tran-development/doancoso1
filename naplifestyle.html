POSITION

Nap Luxury Guest House is located on the third floor of an elegant historic building located
in Piazza Bovio, in the heart of the city of Naples.

Naples has many souls. It's an European metropolis, a center of innovation and cultural experimentation, cradle of civilization with a maze of streets and alleyways stratified upon millenia of history. Naples it's passionate, exuberant, extreme. It throbs with life, it's the sound of the many facets of this city. To live Naples it's exciting, understanding it's wonderful. Its art, music and food are opportunities to discover one of the most eclectic citiy in Europe.

Naples has been breeding ground of great artists and intellectuals, and the city still preserves the memory of his magnificent history in many views of the city, where you can truly understand the spirit and the pride of these people. Naples is the heart of the so-called Campania Felix, a fertile and prosper land, thanks to the ashes of Vesuvio. For Neapolitan people foods is not only nourishment, but culture, sharing and most of all Love! Naples is an ancient city full of charm, where every street and every alley tells us something about its history of great glory, big defeats and domination of foreign population. All of this has contributed to the development of the identity of the Neapolitan people, that is strong and unique.
