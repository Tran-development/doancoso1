On the third floor of an elegant historic building from the nineteenth century, Nap is a guest house located in the heart of Naples, a charming B&B where the unique and stylish decor alongside a service of excellence are designed to meet all guests’ needs.

PIAZZA BOVIO

Impressive square from the late nineteenth century, in the heart of Naples, was the hot spot of the great cultural and artistic movement that led to the city renewal. His metro stop, University, designed by Karim Rashid, is among the Art Stations that make the Naples metro line of the most beautiful in Europe.

ROOMS

Four bedrooms, four different souls, the same passion for the
refined detail, for precious materials, for the authentic Neapolitan
style that shows itself in warm and elegant welcome.

Living and Breakfast

The living room is 35 mq, elegant and welcoming, which houses the reception and the breakfast area. During the day the common areas can provide a comfortable place to spend some times of peace and relax.

The international breakfast offers dishes both sweet and salty and takes place in the living areas from 7.30 to 9.00. If you wish, room service is available, for a sweet start of the day.

SURROUNDINGS

Nap Luxury Guest House is the center of a rich journey made of wonderful places to discover and experience. Within minutes from the "Maschio Angioino", the Angevin Castle, and "Spaccanapoli", the heart of the historic center, it is perfectly connected to the rest of the city with the underground stop "University", placed right in Piazza Bovio, one of the "Art Station" among the most beautiful in Europe.
